About WeatherWeb

Your reliable companion for accurate, real-time weather forecasts and conditions everywhere around the globe.

target Our Mission

To provide beautiful, intuitive, and accurate weather data accessible to everyone. We believe checking the weather should be as refreshing as a clear blue sky, which is why we focus heavily on user experience and reliable data sourcing.

data_usage Where we get our data?

WeatherWeb is proudly powered by the Open-Meteo API. Open-Meteo provides free, high-performance weather forecast APIs, blending data from numerous national weather services across the globe to deliver highly precise weather metrics right to your screen.
